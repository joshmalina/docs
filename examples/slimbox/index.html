Slimbox is a lightbox that works with Mootools

Source: http://www.digitalia.be/software/slimbox

See Demo: Load Image

Download: slimbox1.71.zip
That zip includes:
  • slimbox.js
  • slimbox.css
  • closelabel.gif
  • loading.gif

  • prevlabel.gif
Instructions:

1. Download the above zip, unzip it and move 6 files (js, css and images) into a folder on your server.
